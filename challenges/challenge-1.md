# Challenge 1: Write and Publish a Blog Post

> This challenge is not intended to be completed in the workshop time.

# Instructions

Pick a topic you find interesting—it could be your mini blog idea from the workshop,
something you've been meaning to write about for a while, or something you learned
recently that you think others would find useful.

Write and publish a blog post about it.

How you get there is up to you, but the workshop covered everything you need: finding
an idea, writing a draft, getting feedback, and sharing your work. Trust the process.

> Remember: perfect is the enemy of the posted. Get something out there, share it, and
> take what you learn into the next one.

Here are some great resources for helping you write and publish your first blog post:

- *[Writing for Developers: Blogs That Get Read](https://www.manning.com/books/writing-for-developers)*
  by [Piotr Sarna](https://bio.sarna.dev/) & [Cynthia Dunlop](https://github.com/scynthiadunlop)
- ...and their blog about blogging: [writethat.blog](https://writethat.blog/)
- *[Write More Blog Articles Not Fewer: Don't Leave the Scraps On the Cutting Floor](https://rmoff.net/2025/03/11/write-more-blog-articles-not-fewer-dont-leave-the-scraps-on-the-cutting-floor/)*
  by [Robin Moffatt](https://rmoff.net/)
